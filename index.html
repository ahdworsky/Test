About SkySure

SkySure Inspections helps buyers, sellers, homeowners, and real estate agents gain confidence in the roof—one of the most expensive components of a home. Frequently Asked Questions

  • How long does a Roof Condition Report take?

    Most roof reports take 30–45 minutes onsite, with your full report delivered within 24 hours.

  • Is it safe to fly drones over my home?

    Yes. SkySure is FAA Part 107 Certified and follows strict safety protocols and flight regulations to protect your property.

  • Can I use your roof report for an insurance claim?

    Absolutely. Our reports include high-resolution images and detailed notes that insurers accept to support storm-damage or repair claims.

  • Why not just have a roofer inspect my roof?

    Roofers are great for repairs, but may miss small issues—or focus on what they can sell. SkySure provides an unbiased, third-party Roof Condition Report so you know the truth before committing to repairs.

  • How much does a Roof Condition Report cost?

    Pricing depends on your preferred frequency, but most roof reports are a fraction of the cost of roof repairs—a small investment that can save thousands by catching problems early.
